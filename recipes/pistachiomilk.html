Pistachio Milk

Description

This creamy pistachio milk is easy to make with a high powered blender and a long soak. I add a teaspoon of vanilla when I use this milk for smoothies or for coffee drinks.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted shelled pistachios
  • 4 cups filtered water

Steps

  1. Add pistachios to a large bowl with enough water to cover. Soak overnight; drain.
  2. Place pistachios and 4 cups filtered water in a high powered blender, such as a VitamixÂ®. Blend until smooth and creamy.
